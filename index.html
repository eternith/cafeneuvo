About Cafe Neuvo

Coffee matters with us. Originally from Europe, we came here with a mission to bring the love of coffee to Vancouver. We believe a good cup starts with a cup of care and that philosophy is behind everything we serve here. Come here to discover what makes us unique. Your coffee is waiting. We are proud to offer you a careful selection of in-house made food including sandwiches, salads, soups, and baked goods. They are made with mostly organic, locally sourced fresh ingredients and the condiments used are made from scratch in-house.
