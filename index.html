About Cafe Neuvo

Coffee matters with us. Originally from Europe, we came here with a mission to bring the love of coffee to Vancouver. We believe a good cup starts with a cup of care and that philosophy is behind everything we serve here. Come here to discover what makes us unique. Your coffee is waiting.

We are proud to share our homemade made lattes and baked goods. The recipes have been passed down through generations and we are honoured to make them a part of our cafe. All ingredients are organically selected, with no animal cruelty involved. Here at Cafe Neuvo, we believe in a sustainable Earth, so we do not provide straws unless requested.
